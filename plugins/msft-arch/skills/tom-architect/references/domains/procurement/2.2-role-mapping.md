## Position to role mapping & sizing by function

> **Architect Notes**: This asset aligns your current positions with future roles, connects these roles to process areas, and helps us clearly understand who does what and what the security requirements are for each role. We will use this to drive security discussions with you as we plan application build, testing and training activities. We will also use this to plan the size and service delivery model for each role. In the end, we will have a clear and realistic picture of your future workforce and talent needs, segregation of duties, and security requirements.
