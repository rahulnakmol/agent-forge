> **Architect Notes**: The Reporting Packages & Dashboards asset is a matrix of key reports and dashboards which offer deep insights into key metrics and performance indicators. There are three different types of reports, including standard reports, Powered reports, and custom reports. Standard Reports are out of the box reports. Powered Reports are pre-built reports in the pre-configured environments; and Custom Reports refer to any reports we custom-build for you in addition to the Standard and Powered reports. Together, these reports will provide a clear view of your business landscape. In this example, we can see various reports organized by process all the way down to the task level. In addition to a description of the report, the priority and report types are indicated. There are also columns showing which application is generating the report, the frequency of report generation, data security levels, and KPIs associated with each report.
