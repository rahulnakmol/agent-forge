## Prepare Production process flow in ARIS

> **Architect Notes**: Most of our TOM assets are housed in ARIS, which is a robust enterprise modeling tool and a key component of our Powered execution suite. In addition to the process flows on the right, you can quickly navigate to different processes using the index on the left. The model for each subcategory is listed on the right. This small black and white icon indicates that you can drill down to a deeper level. Let’s drill down into Prepare Production.

## Prepare production process flow

> **Architect Notes**: This is the process flow for prepare production. Each of the yellow boxes is a functional role and the tasks they perform are represented by green boxes on the right. These swim lanes make it easy to see who is responsible for each task. Tasks and processes are indexed so that you can easily see where they fit in the overall taxonomy. They are also linked by gateway markers that indicate if a task is performed in parallel or in sequence. The task boxes have a lot of information built in. Let’s zoom into a couple tasks to take a closer look.

## Role Based Process Flows – Tasks

> **Architect Notes**: A red CP at the top right indicates that this has a control point. A red O in the bottom left means that this activity is performed manually in the software system. A blue “A” indicates that the activity is automated, and a red M indicates that the task is performed outside of the system manually. A teal R on the top left indicates that there is a report associated with this activity.
