> **Architect Notes**: Here we can see the taxonomy for the Measure to Report Process. This taxonomy has 7 level 2 processes shown in dark blue. The level 3 processes are shown below in light blue. If we drill down to the next layer, we get to the actual process flows showing which tasks are performed by whom and in what order. Process taxonomies serve as a framework for the end-to-end processes in each function. When we kick off your transformation, we will use these to help define the scope of the engagement. We will add missing processes and remove irrelevant processes to customize these taxonomies to your organization. We will continue to use this as a heat map to capture your pain points. These customized taxonomies will standardize how our teams talk about processes across your organization.
