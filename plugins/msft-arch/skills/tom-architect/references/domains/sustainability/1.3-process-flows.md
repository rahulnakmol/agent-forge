## Prepare External ESG Reporting process flow in ARIS

> **Architect Notes**: This is the process flow for preparing external ESG reporting. Each of the yellow boxes on the left indicates a functional role and the tasks they perform are represented by green boxes on the right. These swim lanes make it easy to see who is responsible for each task. Tasks and processes are indexed so that you can easily see where they fit in the overall taxonomy. They are also linked by gateway markers that indicate if a task is performed in parallel or in sequence. The task boxes have a lot of information built in.
