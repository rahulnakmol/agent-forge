> **Architect Notes**: It is structured in the same format as the process taxonomy and depicts the executive owners responsible for each end-to-end process. They are the ones who oversee process performance and are ultimately accountable for the design and governance of the end-to-end process. This asset clarifies who owns each process down to the second level. When reviewing this taxonomy, we can insert the Global Process Owner who oversees each process from start to finish on this line. In some cases, one Global Process Owner oversees the entire taxonomy. However, if there are multiple Global Process Owners, this overlay asset will help us clarify who is responsible for each process.
