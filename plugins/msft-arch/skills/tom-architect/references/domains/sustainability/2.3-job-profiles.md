> **Architect Notes**: This asset contains detailed job profiles for each functional position. Each profile describes the roles and responsibilities of each position and includes required skills, capabilities, critical success factors, education and experience. This information gives us a clear picture of what each position does and how it adds value to your organization in your new target operating model. For example, we have two positions listed here: a Master Data Manager and an ESG Reporting analyst. To the left, we can see they support the IT and Sustainability office, respectively. As we continue to the right, we see detailed role descriptions for each. We can also see where this role resides in the service delivery model. Under scope, we can see that both roles are full-time and permanent. We can also see the reporting lines and with which roles these positions interface. The Master Data manager has 2 direct reports – Master data analyst and a data modeler. Each role has very specific responsibilities, critical success factors, and education and experience requirements.
