> **Architect Notes**: The Supporting Technology Overlay is a tech specific asset. It is an end-to-end process taxonomy that shows the underlying technology applications that enable each level 3 process. It gives us details on the application modules we will need to best enable the end-to-end process. Our platform-specific assets are enabled through a variety of technology platforms, including Oracle, SAP, Workday, Microsoft, and other leading vendors. In this example, we can explore the SAP tools that can optimize the Measure to Report process flows by viewing this supporting technology overlay. The “Collect and Prepare ESG Data from Systems” level 3 process is performed in SAP Sustainability Control Tower, while the “Prepare External ESG Reporting” process is performed in SAP Disclosure Management. And lastly “Review Performance and Review ESG Related Risks” process is performed using two modules: SAP Sustainability Tower and SAP S/4 HANA.
