# 0.2 TOM Introduction — Intro

## Target Operating Model

Target Operating Model
Introduction

> **Architect Notes**: I would like to introduce you to the Target Operating Model. In your business transformation, the Target Operating Model, or TOM, is a great place to start. The TOM is a blueprint for how your organization will operate in the future. It defines what 'good' looks like and enables us to accelerate your journey toward your desired future state. While the target operating model is comprised of leading practices, it is not a “one size fits all” model. It’s a starting point. As we go through operations with you, we can identify together which processes you might need to change or add to your customized Target Operating Model. You will have this model to guide you through your transformation, and it is yours to keep even after our engagement has been completed. In other words, the tools that I share with you today will be customized and will become your tools.
