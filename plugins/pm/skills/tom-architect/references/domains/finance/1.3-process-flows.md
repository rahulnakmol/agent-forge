## Recording recurring journal entries process flow

> **Architect Notes**: This is the process flow for recording recurring journal entries. Each of the yellow boxes is a functional role and the tasks they perform are represented by green boxes on the right. These swim lanes make it easy to see who is responsible for each task. Tasks and processes are indexed so that you can easily see where they fit in the overall taxonomy. They are also linked by gateway markers that indicate if a task is performed in parallel or in sequence. The task boxes have a lot of information built in. Let’s zoom into a couple tasks to take a closer look.

## Role Based Process Flows – Tasks

> **Architect Notes**: The red CP at the top right indicates that this has a control point. The red O in the bottom left means that this activity is performed manually in the software system. A blue “A” would indicate that the activity is automated, and a red M would indicate that the task is performed outside of the system manually. The teal R you see on the top left indicates that there is a report associated with this activity.
