## Functional position job profiles

> **Architect Notes**: This asset contains detailed job profiles for each functional position. Each profile describes the roles and responsibilities of each position and includes required skills, capabilities, critical success factors, education and experience. This information gives us a clear picture of what each position does and how it adds value to your organization in your new target operating model. For example, we have two positions listed here: an accounting analyst and an accounts payable analyst. To the left, we can which processes they help to execute. To the right, we see detailed role descriptions for each. We can also see where this role resides in the service delivery model. Under scope, we can see that both roles are full-time. We can also see the reporting lines and with which roles these positions interface. Neither position has direct reports, but each role has very specific responsibilities, critical success factors, and education and experience requirements.
