> **Architect Notes**: The Service Delivery Model Overlay is a one-page taxonomy overlay that depicts the optimal nature of the work being performed for a given process area. It is based on the Service Delivery Model Framework, which classifies client business processes as services. It provides a leading practice point of view on the delivery model of those services. It is typically used to develop a case for change.

## Process Area Service Delivery Model

> **Architect Notes**: In addition to the one-page taxonomy overlay available in PowerPoint, a more detailed view of the Service Delivery Model is available in a spreadsheet like this. This view helps us to identify if work should be sourced internally or by a third-party externally. It also helps us work with our clients to identify the optimal location to perform the work, which can vary based on unique client circumstances.
