> **Architect Notes**: The Supporting Technology Overlay is a tech specific asset. It is an end-to-end process taxonomy that shows the underlying technology applications that enable each level 3 process. It gives us details on the application modules we will need to best enable the end-to-end process. Our platform-specific assets are enabled through a variety of technology platforms, including Oracle, SAP, Workday, Microsoft, and other leading vendors. In this example, we can explore the Workday tools that can optimize the Workforce Administration process flows by viewing this supporting technology overlay. The “Hire Worker in System” process is performed in Workday Core HCM, while the “Administer Worker’s Compensation” process is performed in Workday Help. The “Maintain Organization Structure” process is performed using two systems: Workday Core HCM and Workday Help.
