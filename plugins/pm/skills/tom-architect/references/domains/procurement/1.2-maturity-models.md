Also, achieving a 5 rating in every category is not necessarily advisable. It takes a great amount of focus and resources to get the highest rating. ## What’s the gap? How do we close it?

The business is not doing well in handling the complexity of the business using the current technology, and further functionality could be leveraged to bring baseline benefits.
Finance will challenge and change ways of working, and therefore it is not looking to an SAP implementation.
Getting staff trained has been a challenge in the past.
As-Is
Finance will work collaboratively with Business Units to determine the right technology that best supports the end-to-end processes.
Finance will have a sound understanding of the end-to-end process with technology having the right functionality creating an efficient, a customer focused and an agile function.
Opportunities to maximize technology by expanding functionalities will be identified.
To-Be
Right technology with the right functionalities, supporting the end-to-end processes, creating a Finance function that is efficient, customer-focused and agile.
