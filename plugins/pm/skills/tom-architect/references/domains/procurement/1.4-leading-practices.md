| Develop service delivery model | The current state service delivery model is assessed and potential gaps as well as opportunities are identified in order for a future state service delivery model to be developed (includes such areas as, but not limited to, activities, outputs, locations, channels). | Establish governance framework and procurement policies | The organizations management, communications and reporting structure are defined. This supports the definition of role authority and accountability (Delegate of Authority). | Improves alignment to corporate strategy and/or organizational structure |
| Determine skills, capacity & capabilities needed for functional & operational activities | Defines the core competencies, skills and capabilities required of resources (human capital and automation). Develops a resource plan which meets the needs and requirements of the procurement strategy, both current and future state. | Improves alignment to corporate strategy and/or organizational structure |

> **Architect Notes**: Design considerations are significant decisions a client makes when designing a platform solution. This Leading Practices asset provides a specific point of view on how something should be designed and what benefits result from that design decision. It shows where in the process the impact is felt and in which software module the design should be considered. It also identifies which leading practices may have ESG implications.
