> **Architect Notes**: The Service Management Framework asset is part of the Service Delivery Model layer of the Target Operating Model. It follows the Service Delivery Model, which defines the what work is being performed based on the characteristics of the activities, who performs the activities, and where the activities are performed. As a complementary asset, the Service Management Framework indicates how services are managed to optimize business value.

## A “mini-Target Operating Model” for Shared Service Centers

> **Architect Notes**: The service management framework asset contains the necessary supporting processes and infrastructure required to setup and implement a Shared Services, Center of Expertise or Corporate services model. The Service Management Framework is often considered a "mini-Target Operating Model."
