> **Architect Notes**: The Supporting Technology Overlay is a tech specific asset. It is an end-to-end process taxonomy that shows the underlying technology applications that enable each level 3 process. It gives us details on the application modules we will need to best enable the end-to-end process. Our platform-specific assets are enabled through a variety of technology platforms, including Oracle, SAP, Workday, Microsoft, and other leading vendors. In this example, we can explore the Oracle tools that can optimize the Schedule to Produce process flows by viewing this supporting technology overlay. The “Material Requirements Planning” process is performed in the Oracle Supply Chain Planning module, while most of the “Execute Production Schedule” process is performed in the Oracle Manufacturing module. The exception is the “Transfer of Produced Goods” process, which is performed using two systems: An external system as well as the Oracle Inventory Management module.
