> **Architect Notes**: The Security and Controls asset is comprised of two components. The first is a matrix of key risks and controls. This asset articulates key risks and identifies the controls that should be implemented to mitigate those risks to form an effective control environment.

> **Architect Notes**: The second is a list of segregation of duties and sensitive access risks. A segregation of duties risk is when a combination of abilities that, when assigned to a back-end user, constitutes a risk. A sensitive or critical access risk is where the direct assignment to a back-end user constitutes a risk. The objective of this tool is to facilitate the appropriate division of responsibilities and to ensure that access is restricted to the right individuals. Establishing appropriate security and controls is important to all companies, but it is especially relevant to public companies and companies considering an IPO.
