## Policies

> **Architect Notes**: The Policies asset is a list of key policies that support effective operational governance of processes and systems. It can be used as a checklist of policies recommended to have within your policy inventory. During our engagement, we will review this list of recommended policies, determine which are applicable to you, customize it to fit your organization, and identify global process owners.
